These are some of the fabulous animes that are worth watching

Most of them aren't main-stream animes

Assassination Classroom

Lessons

    1. Everyone has special abilities.

    2. Anything can be a sword if you polish it enough.

    3. One can become stronger by acknowledging their weaknesses

    4. It's ok to fail but one should never give up

    5. Don't concern yourself with the opinions of others.

Classroom of the Elite

Lessons

    1. Equailty is just a fantasy,the world runs on meritocracy.

    2. Everyone is a moon and has a dark side,which they never shows to anyone.

    3. Every individual has his/her own strength & weaknesses,that's what it means to be a human.

    4. One have to give explanations for their actions,either to themselves or to someone else

    5. "Truth' is a weapon that can be acquired through the accumulation of "Trust".

Your lie in April

Lessons

    1. Maybe there's only a dark road up ahead. But one still have to believe and keep going.

    2. Music is freedom.

    3. Setbacks are inevitable to superstars. Adversity is what separates the good from the great. After all, stars can only shine during the night.

    4. Autumn shows us how beautiful it is sometimes to let things go
